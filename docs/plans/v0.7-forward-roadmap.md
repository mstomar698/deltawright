## Forward plan — 12 functionality items, prioritized, in 3 waves

Sizes: **S** = days, **M** = ~1 week, **L** = multi-week. Every item lists its risk to the guards
(DW-02 verdict authority, DW-03 unsure-is-first-class) and to the non-goal.

## Recommendation

Ship **Wave 1 as a "v0.7.x Trust & Adoption hardening" milestone**, and queue the **offline trace
reader (#9) as the v0.8 flagship**.

The whole positioning is "the single **accurate** place," yet the three floors run only in a local smoke
test and both releases were hand-cut — so today the central trust claim is **unenforced** and every
publish is manual-risk. Wave 1 is ~pure integration over already-shipped data (`aggregate`'s
`FlakeReport`, `checksum`, the zero-edit reporter): it turns the floors into a hard CI gate, makes cutting
a version one action (OIDC removes the OTP pain), and makes the flake-triage story visible where teams
look (a dashboard artifact + a PR comment). It carries ~zero risk to DW-02/DW-03 or the observe-only
non-goal, ships in days, and lays the CI/release rails every later wave rides on. Do **not** ship a Size-L
reach feature onto an unenforced accuracy gate — that is building reach on an unguarded floor.
